Refund Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026 · Version 1.0

This page explains how and when Navigator Trading Systems (operator of navigatoralgo.com) issues refunds. It applies to Paddle-processed purchases (the annual Signal Provider subscription and monthly extra-slot add-ons). Purchases made through MQL5 Market are governed by the MQL5 Market refund policy in addition to anything written here.

1. First-charge guarantee — 7 days, no questions asked

Your very first Paddle charge on any new subscription is refundable in full, no questions asked, within 7 calendar days of purchase.

This covers:

  • The $100/year Signal Provider annual subscription (first year only).
  • The first $8/month extra-slot charge for a given provider account.

To claim it, email support@navigatoralgo.com or message @Navigatoralgo_bot with your Paddle order number. We will cancel the subscription immediately and instruct Paddle to refund your original payment method. Expect the refund to arrive in 5-10 business days depending on your bank / card issuer.

2. After the first charge

Following renewal charges (second year of provider subscription, second month onwards of extra-slot add-ons) are not automatically refundable. This is standard for subscription software: by continuing past the first billing cycle, you are consenting to the ongoing service.

You can cancel at any time from your dashboard. Cancellation stops the next renewal charge; the current paid period continues until it naturally ends, and you keep full access until then.

3. Exceptions — case-by-case refunds

We will consider a pro-rated or full refund for a renewal charge if any of the following apply:

  • Service outage. Navigator Algo was down for more than 12 consecutive hours during the paid period due to a fault on our side, confirmed by our status page / Firebase incident log.
  • Charge you didn't authorize. Someone charged your card using stolen credentials. Report to us and to Paddle immediately.
  • Duplicate charge / billing error on our side. Always refunded, full amount.
  • Promised feature not delivered. If we pre-announced a feature on a specific billing tier and did not deliver, you can request a refund for the tier upgrade.

4. What is not refundable

Trading losses are never refundable. Navigator Algo is a signal-relay infrastructure platform. We do not provide trading advice, we do not execute trades on your broker, and we do not guarantee any signal provider's performance. A losing week or a bad signal from a provider you chose to follow is not grounds for refund from us.

We also do not refund:

  • Charges where your license was revoked for breach of the Terms of Service (shared license, reverse engineering, rate-limit abuse, etc.).
  • Charges where you filed a chargeback before contacting us to resolve the issue. Chargebacks without prior contact can also result in account termination.
  • MT5 Receiver EA purchases sold through MQL5 Market — those go through MQL5's own refund process.
  • Partial months / unused slots when you cancel — access continues until end of period, and that's the refund in kind.

5. Chargebacks

If you believe you have been incorrectly charged, contact us first. 99% of disputes are simply misunderstandings that we resolve in a day or two. Filing a chargeback with your card issuer before contacting us causes:

  • Immediate suspension of your provider account and any active licenses until the dispute is resolved.
  • A $15 chargeback-handling fee (charged by Paddle, passed to us) that we may recover from you.
  • Potential permanent ban from using navigatoralgo.com.

6. How to request a refund

  1. Email support@navigatoralgo.com from the email on your Paddle receipt, OR message @Navigatoralgo_bot.
  2. Include: your Paddle order / subscription ID, the date of the charge, the amount, and (optionally) the reason. For exception refunds (§3), give us enough detail to verify.
  3. We respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed through Paddle and arrive in 5-10 business days on your original payment method.
  4. If we deny your request, we will explain why in writing. You always retain your rights under applicable consumer law (including the EU 14-day cooling-off period for EU consumers, which is wider than our 7-day window and overrides it for EU customers).

7. EU / UK consumers — statutory right of withdrawal

If you are a consumer resident in the EU or UK, you have a 14-day cooling-off period from the date of purchase during which you may cancel a distance-purchase of digital services and request a full refund, under the EU Consumer Rights Directive (2011/83/EU) and the UK Consumer Contracts Regulations.

By actively using the dashboard / minting licenses / running signals during the first 14 days, you expressly waive this right with respect to the portion of the service you have already consumed. We will still refund any unused remainder of your first billing cycle if you cancel within 14 days.

8. Questions

Email support@navigatoralgo.com. We answer within 2 business days, usually faster.
