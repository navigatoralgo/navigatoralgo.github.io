// FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Does the $100 include a receiver EA for my subscribers?
Yes. Your purchase generates 10 free SUB- license codes. Your subscribers download the free Partner Receiver EA from this page and paste one of your codes in — it binds to their MT5 account on first activation. No MQL5 Market purchase required for them.
What happens when I run out of 10 slots?
You can revoke inactive slots from the dashboard and reissue them to new subscribers. Additional subscribers beyond 10 can buy the paid Receiver EA on MQL5 ($30/3mo) and follow you directly — no slot needed on your side. Extra slot packs (+10 at $20) are on the roadmap.
How fast do trades copy?
Typically 1–3 seconds end-to-end. Receivers use adaptive polling — 2 seconds when signals are flowing, 10 seconds when quiet. Firebase Realtime Database is our transport; it's designed for this.
Will my broker's symbol names work on my subscriber's broker?
The Receiver EA includes a 4-layer resolver: exact match → suffix scan (m, .pro, #, etc) → alias map → manual override. In practice it works across 95%+ of broker pairs out of the box.
Can my subscribers run this on a prop firm account?
Yes — the Receiver EA has per-symbol skip lists, per-magic skip lists, max-risk-per-trade, fixed-lot override, and a "no trailing" switch. Subscribers can tune it to fit FTMO, MyForexFunds, FundedNext, etc. rules before their evaluation starts.
Is my Firebase data private?
Yes. Each provider node is locked by Firebase security rules — only the authenticated owner can read their own sub-licenses, copier list, and stats. Receivers can only read the signals stream, never anything else.
What if I stop paying the $100/yr?
Your provider license goes inactive, broadcasting stops, and your sub-license codes stop working. Subscribers on the paid Receiver plan are unaffected structurally but have nothing to copy until you renew.
Can I run multiple MT5 accounts from one provider license?
One license binds to one MT5 account number on activation. If you trade on multiple accounts and want to broadcast each, you can buy additional licenses. Most providers run one master account and that's it.
